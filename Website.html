Welcome to Education


The Pamantasan ng Cabuyao (PnC) is a public educational institution in Cabuyao,
Laguna established in 2003 through the Municipal Ordinance No. 2003-059.
Pamantasan ng Cabuyao offers a wide variety of courses under its colleges of Education, Arts and Sciences, Business Administration and Accountancy, Engineering, and Computer Studies. Its School of Technical and Vocational Education (STEVE)
offers training in Tech-Voc fields such as Caregiving, Automotive Servicing,
Cookery, Bookkeeping, and Electronic Product Assembly Servicing.
Another program that the school offering is a DepEd-recognized Senior High School (SHS) program featuring strands under the Academic and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) tracks. The university has a rich student culture, having a number of academic organizations and clubs that help in the student’s overall development.
